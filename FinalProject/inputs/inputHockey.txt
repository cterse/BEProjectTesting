A hockey league is made up of at least four hockey teams. Each hockey team is 
composed of six to twelve players and one player captains the hockey team. A hockey team has 
a name and a record. Players have a number and a position. Hockey teams play 
games against each other. Each game has a score and a location. Hockey teams are taught by a coach. 
A coach has a level of accreditation and a number of years of experience. 
A coach can teach multiple teams. A player and a coach are people. People have name.
