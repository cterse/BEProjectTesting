A hockey league is made up of at least four hockey teams.
Each hockey team is  composed of 6 to 12 players.
A team has a name.
A team has a record.
Players have a position.
Players have a number.
Teams play games against each other.
Each game has a score.
Each game has a location.
Teams are taught by a coach.
A coach has a number of years of experience.
A coach has a level of accreditation.
A coach can teach multiple teams.
Players are people.
People have names.
